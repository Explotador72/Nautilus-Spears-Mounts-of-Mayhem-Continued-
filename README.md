=== MOUNTS OF MAYHEM — CONTINUITY FIX ===
A maintained fork of Gospi's Mounts of Mayhem, with bugs fixed and the original discontinued version brought back to life.
<details>
<summary>EN</summary>
Backports the Mounts of Mayhem drop content to Minecraft 1.21.1. Spears, rideable Nautilus mounts, Nautilus Armor, zombie variants, and more. Combat, exploration, and underwater travel expanded.
== BUGS FIXED ==
- Nautilus Dash: no longer glitches or fails to trigger
- Nautilus movement speed: increased to match its role as a fast mount
- Armor visibility: armor now renders correctly while mounted
- Spear attack penalty: attacking no longer slows the player down
